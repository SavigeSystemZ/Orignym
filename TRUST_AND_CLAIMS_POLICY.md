# Orignym - Trust and Claims Policy

## 1. Core Principle: No Legal Ownership
**CRITICAL:** Orignym does **not** grant legal ownership of words. It is a registry for recording claims, timestamping them, and searching for prior use.

No public-facing copy, notification, or AI-generated response may ever imply:
- Guaranteed originality.
- Trademark clearance or validity.
- Exclusive legal rights to a word.

## 2. Acceptable Terminology
- **Do Use:** recorded, claimed, timestamped, evidence-backed, low-conflict finding, prior-use signals found, verification run completed, published claim page.
- **Do NOT Use:** legally owned word, guaranteed original, accredited ownership, exclusive rights granted, trademark cleared, globally unique.

## 3. Transparency & Limitations
Every published claim and verification verdict must be accompanied by a **limitations note**. This note must clearly explain that:
- A "no strong conflict found" verdict simply means no conflict was detected *in the sources checked at the time of verification*.
- It does not guarantee the word has never been used elsewhere.
