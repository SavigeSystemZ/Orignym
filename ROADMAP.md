# Roadmap

This file defines the strategic sequence of major milestones. The active execution plan lives in `PLAN.md`.

## Near term

- **Post-v1.0 Maintenance:** Monitoring initial production traffic and AI performance.
- **Mobile App Refinement:** Polishing the Flutter module for Android release.

## Medium term

- **Blockchain Anchoring:** Implementing tamper-evident hash proofs on a distributed ledger.
- **Advanced ML Fine-tuning:** Training custom models on verified claim feedback loops.

## Long term

- **Global IP Ecosystem:** Expanding legal integrations to 50+ jurisdictions.
- **Decentralized Governance:** Shifting moderation from centralized admins to token-weighted community consensus.

## Completed

- **M0-M12:** System Foundation, Data Model, AI Verification, Suggestion Engine, Publish & Registry, Moderation, Distribution, Hardening, Advanced Search, Personalization, Collaboration/API, and Community/Analytics.
