# Orignym - Moderation Policy

## 1. Core Objective
Maintain the integrity of the Orignym registry by preventing fraud, impersonation, bad-faith squatting, spam, and abusive content.

## 2. Reporting & Disputes
Users and visitors can report published entries or challenge a claim via a structured dispute flow.
- **Reports:** For spam, offensive terms, or clear violations of Terms of Service.
- **Disputes:** For challenging a claim based on prior use, bad-faith registration, or trademark infringement.

## 3. Moderation Actions
Administrators have access to a moderation queue with the following capabilities:
- **Freeze:** Temporarily hide a published claim while under review.
- **Unpublish:** Permanently revert a claim to draft status or mark it rejected.
- **Ban/Suspend:** Block malicious users from creating new claims.

## 4. Auditability
- All moderation actions are reversible.
- Every state change, report, and admin action is logged immutably in the `AuditEvent` table.
